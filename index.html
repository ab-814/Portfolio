About Me

Hi! I'm Alex Burns, a web developer living in Brooklyn. I have over three years of experience building websites and applications for clients of Cognizant Technology Solutions. These clients covered a wide range of industries, locations and corporate environments, and their technology needs were even more diverse.

I enjoy flexing my creative muscle and I'm passionate about innovative and stylish design. I want to work with motivated people who appreciate good teamwork, and leadership that takes care of the people who work for them. What sets me apart is my ability to connect and communicate with people of different backgrounds and personalities. I have a thorough understanding of team dynamics and an even-keeled attitude that makes me a valuable addition to any team.
